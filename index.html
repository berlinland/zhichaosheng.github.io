Biography

    Sheng Zhichao, a Distinguished Associate Researcher at the School of Communication and Information Engineering, Shanghai University.
    Obtained doctoral degrees from Shanghai University and University of Technology Sydney in 2017 and 2018 respectively.
    Worked as an Algorithm Engineer at ZTE Corporation from December 2017 to August 2018. Engaged in postdoctoral research at Queen's University Belfast in the United Kingdom from September 2018 to September 2019.
    Authored over twenty papers in domestic and international academic journals and conferences. As a first author, published multiple papers in IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing and IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications.
    Also serves as a reviewer for journals such as IEEE JSAC, TIFS, TSP, TWC, TCOM, and TVT. Member of IEEE and IEEE Communications Society.
